About Me

I am senior data scientist with the National Research Council Digital Technologies. As part of the NRC’s Data Analytics Centre, I lead initiatives aiming to accelerate the adoption of data analytics and AI in the public service.

Before, I worked with the Centre for Operational Research and Analysis of Defence Research and Development Canada, where I became the first-ever Director of Data Science in the Department of National Defence (DND) and built a data science team supporting the Chief Data Officer and Assistant Deputy Minister (Data, Innovation, Analytics). I also chaired a NATO Specialist Team on Advanced Analytics and AI and led several operational research projects while working with various branches of DND. I also worked for two years in the UK as an exchange scientist with Dstl.
